This notice sets out the Cookies and similar technologies ("Cookies") used on www.lashaeglamour.co.uk

You can turn Cookies off using your browser settings and sometimes by visiting the site of the individual cookie provider. Please see our Privacy Notice for further information on the personal data we process.

what are cookies?

Cookies are useful tools which are used to store and read information from a website user's browser or device. They are used to help you navigate and use our Site efficiently and perform certain functions which are useful to us as a business and for the services we provide to our customers.

Cookies are used when your browser loads any one of our Sites. Our Site sends information to the user's browser and/or retrieves information from existing Cookies as users navigate through. For example, a website owner can set a cookie to a user's browser to remember their website preferences, when a user returns to the website later, the browser retrieves and sends this file to the website owner to ensure the user's preferences are loaded without requiring the user to re-enter their preference settings. Other Cookies may be used to provide the website owner with useful information about their website or the services they offer to their customers.

For more information, please read the ICO's guidance on Cookies accessible here: https://ico.org.uk/your-data-matters/online/cookies/.

our use of cookies

The Cookies used on our Sites/App all fall within the following four categories:

1. Strictly Necessary; 2. Analytical / Performance; 3. Functionality; and 4. Targeting.

1. strictly necessary cookies

These Cookies are required for the safe operation of the Sites/App and use of all its features. For these Cookies we do not require your consent. They include, for example, Cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of the Site and use a shopping basket. If these are disabled, then some of the necessary website functionality will be lost.

2. functionality cookies

These are used to provide added functionality to our Sites/App, these Cookies are used to recognise you when you return to the Sites/App, the number of visitors to the Sites/App, where visitors have come from and the pages visited. This enables us to personalise our content for you, greet you by name and remember your preferences (for example, your choice of language or region). Such analysis may be conducted by third parties in which case they are considered as third-party Cookies. To use these Cookies, we require your consent.

3. analytical/performance cookies

These Cookies are required for the safe operation of the Sites/App and use of all its features. For these Cookies we do not require your consent. They include, for example, Cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of the Site and use a shopping basket. If these are disabled, then some of the necessary website functionality will be lost.

4. targeting cookies

These Cookies are placed by third party advertising companies and collect information about your browsing habits to display advertising more relevant to your interests based on your online navigation. We may also share this information with third parties for this purpose or to inform our future marketing campaigns and target bases. For example, we use these Cookies to:

• Show you interest-based advertising following a visit on our Website.

• Provide third party advertising companies and social networks (such as Facebook or Twitter) information about your visit on our Website so that they can show you interest-based advertising;

• Control advertising effectiveness through tracking lead conversion (track that you have made a purchase after following a link to our Website from another third party website).

To use these Cookies, we require your consent.

managing cookies

The storage of Cookies on your device depends on your choices, except for the operating and security Cookies that are necessary for the proper functioning of the Site. Cookies are stored, based on your preferences. You may freely express and change your preferences by clicking here.

You can also block most Cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse the setting of all or some Cookies. However, if you use your browser settings to block all Cookies (including strictly necessary / essential Cookies) you may not be able to access all or parts of the Sites/App.

If you're not sure of the type and version of web browser, you use to access the Internet:

For PCs: click on 'Help' at the top of your browser window and select the 'About' option

For Macs: with the browser window open, click on the Apple menu and select the 'About' option

To learn more about Cookies and how to manage them (including blocking them) visit www.aboutcookies.org.uk. Please note that we are not responsible for the content of any external website.
