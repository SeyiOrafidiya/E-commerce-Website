Customer Care

Orders

How long does nail glue/adhesive tabs last?

Nail glue lasts for up to 2 weeks. Please ensure you prep your natural nails by washing your hands first, filing the surface of your nails and then applying glue but make sure that glue does not seep along the sides of your press on nails.

Adhesive tabs lasts for up to 3 days. These are perfect for day time events and last minute outings. We recommend storing your used press on nails in our eco-friendly packaging to ensure no outside factors (eg. debris and lint) reduces the tackiness of the tabs.

Are the nails reusable?

Our nails are reusable! The longevity of our press on nails depend on your day to day activities. We recommmend following our guidelines to remove your press ons to allow healthy natural nails.

What is included in my nail order?

Every order will come with a nail file and 2g of glue.

Can I make a custom order?

Yes we do take custom orders. Please click Custom Orders first to view what we currently have in our catalogue. If none of our custom sets are to your liking, please email us: help@lashaeglamour.co.uk and include your full name, order number, mobile number and a picture of the nails you would like us to replicate.

Please allow 7 working days for us to create the nails and 2 working days for shipping.

Please note that there is a flat fee to all custom orders. We will endeavour to recreate the nails exactly as requested. Please allow for some discrepencies due to the nature of the product being 100% handmade. We do not allow refunds/exchanges on custom orders.

Sizing

How will I know my size?

We produce 20 nails, all offering differing sizes. 10 nails for 5 fingers will ensure you have more options for your convenience. You are sure to find the correct size for your nails.

Can I choose any shape?

You can choose from Coffin, Stiletto or Almond. We will not charge you for a different shape. Please make sure to put the shape you want into the text box on the check out page.

Shipping and Returns

What are your processing times?

We are open Monday - Friday 9am to 5pm excluding bank holidays. Our processing times are as follows:

Ready to Ship sets: 2-3 working days

Made to Order sets: 5 working days

Custom Sets: 7 working days

When your order is dispatched we will send you an email to confirm this. Delivery will be by Royal Mail and this usually takes 1-2 working days.

Please note that the above time frames are estimates and there may be delays. You will receive an email confirmation at each stage thanks to our fully automated order system.

What is the cancellation and returns policy?

We accept cancellations within 24 hours of purchase.

All Ready To Ship sets can be returned within 14 days from receiving the order. Please contact help@lashaeglamour.co.uk for more information. Please be aware, we do not offer free returns unless the product was incorrect or damaged. We do not allow exchanges on any of our products.

Your request will be processed within 10 business days after we receive your return. The refund will be issued to the original payment account.

Can I still return items if they are worn or damaged?

1. Items must be returned unworn and undamaged and with all original contents intact.

2. Final sales items and free gifts cannot be returned.

3. If you receive a damaged/defective item, please contact help@lashaeglamour.co.uk within 24 hours of receipt.

4. The original shipping fee are non-refundable.
